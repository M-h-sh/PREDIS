Who are we

Registered under registration number 2023/917388/08 PREDIS COMMUNITY FORUM is a non profit organization formed to help the previously disadvantaged communities of the country, through economic and skills development programs. We are working for the previously disadvantaged by connecting our members to the economic grid, by coaching our members to utilise their skillset and experience to benefit themselves.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is PREDIS COMMUNITY FORUM, and what is its mission?

    PREDIS COMMUNITY FORUM is a non-profit organization (Registration Number: 2023/917388/08) dedicated to uplifting previously disadvantaged communities. Our mission is to connect these communities to the economic grid through various economic and skills development programs.

  • What services does PREDIS COMMUNITY FORUM provide?

    PREDIS COMMUNITY FORUM offers a range of services, including recruitment, labor relations, skills development, legal cover, medical cover, funeral cover, credit facility, and collaboration with financial institutions. These services aim to empower and support previously disadvantaged individuals in their journey toward economic participation and success.

  • How does PREDIS COMMUNITY FORUM contribute to economic development?

    PREDIS COMMUNITY FORUM contributes to economic development by connecting members to the economic grid, providing coaching to utilize their skillsets, and offering various support services. The organization also collaborates with companies that form a group operating in a center, creating jobs and opportunities for local disadvantaged communities.
